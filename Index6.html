Cat Care Tips That Actually Work

Follow these basic steps to keep your feline friend happy and healthy:

  1. Choose the right type of litter and litter box placement.
  2. Schedule regular vet checkups and vaccinations.
  3. Provide a balanced diet and clean water daily.
  4. Understand common cat behaviors and how to respond.
  5. Ensure mental and physical stimulation through play.
